## Wrestling’s Giant Footprint: WrestleMania and Indie Chaos

If you thought WrestleMania at Allegiant Stadium was the main event, you’re not paying attention. The city is crawling with indie shows in every half-lit corner between the Strip and the burbs. GCW Spring Break and the infamous GCW Joey Janela’s Clusterfuck take over the [Horseshoe](https://www.caesars.com/horseshoe-las-vegas) and Luxor. DragonGate and the WWE ID Showcase drop in for the diehards.
