The Las Vegas skyline is about to change again. Construction has officially begun on a new mega-resort at the south end of the Strip.

## The Project

The resort will feature a 3,000-room hotel, a 100,000 square-foot casino floor, multiple celebrity-chef restaurants, and a state-of-the-art entertainment venue.

## Timeline

The project is expected to take approximately three years to complete, with a grand opening targeted for late 2028.

## What It Means for Vegas

This development signals continued confidence in Las Vegas as a world-class destination, even as the city diversifies beyond gaming.
