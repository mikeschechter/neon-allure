Downtown Las Vegas is about to get even louder. A brand-new three-day music festival has just been announced, and it's set to take over the Fremont East district this October.

## What We Know So Far

The festival will span multiple stages across several blocks of downtown, with a lineup featuring over 50 artists across genres including electronic, hip-hop, indie, and rock.

## Tickets and Pricing

Early-bird tickets are expected to go on sale next month. Sign up for the newsletter to get first access.

## Impact on Downtown

This is a major win for the ongoing revitalization of downtown Las Vegas, bringing thousands of visitors to the area.
